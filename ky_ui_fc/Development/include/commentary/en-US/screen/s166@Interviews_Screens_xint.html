Employment Period Help.

  • Please list each period of employment with this employer

  • If you worked for this employer on separate occasions, please list them separately.

  • Enter the Date of the first day you performed work for the employer.

  • Enter the Date of the last day you performed work for the employer

  • To add another line, click the ‘Add Period’ button.

  • To remove a line, select the ‘Remove’ checkbox for the corresponding line and click the ‘Remove Period’ button.

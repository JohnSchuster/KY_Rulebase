Account Holder's Name

A person operating an account with a bank is an account holder.
An Individual authorized to perform transactions on behalf of an account
Authorization is provided through signatures placed on file with the bank or company managing the account.

Name of Bank

The name of the financial institution that manages you checking and(or) savings account

Routing Transit Number

A nine digit bank code, used in the United States, which appears on the bottom of negotiable instruments such as checks identifying the financial institution on which it was drawn.
The RTN is used by the Automated Clearing House to process direct deposits, bill payments, and other such automated transfers.

Account Number

A checking account number identifies a specific bank account at a specific banking institution.

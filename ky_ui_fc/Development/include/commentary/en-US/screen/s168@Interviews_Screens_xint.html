Employer Listing Help

  • If you worked for a temporary agency, you must list the temporary agency, not the company to which you were assigned.

  • If you worked for a skilled trade union, list the contractor for which you worked, not the trade union name.

  • To add another line, click the ‘Add Employer’ button.

  • To remove a line, select the ‘Remove’ checkbox for the corresponding line and click the ‘Remove Employer’ button.

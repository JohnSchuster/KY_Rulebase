DCI Customized Non-Separation Screen Help

Help is basically a mini-web page in a separate "panel"

Anything you can do on a web page, you can do in Commentary. Images, Style's, Colors, Layout, etc...

The panel can be in a frame, or some help may be in pop-up separate tabs (thus staying open, but requiring the user to close the tab).

You can include multiple headings

Entire screens can have help (commentary), as can specific attributes/questions.

You can include links, and lists.

Specific words in questions, may provide definitional help "earned wages" for example.

Or the Help panel may provide links, For Example: Kentucky Revised Statutes. List by Section.

Or links to specific definitions. For Example: Covered Employment

Include as much text as you need... authoring something that is actually helpful to the user, is the tricky part... With definitions, Spel Chking can bee imprtant.

While authoring help text, we often find we can simplify the question or situation in the rulebase (reducing the need for help).

In the case of the DCI rulebase, i expect help will be most useful for guidance in the many tricky, subjective, interpretive situations.
